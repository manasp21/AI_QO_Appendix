This website functions as an interactive, digital supplement to the research paper, "Solving tricky quantum optics problems with assistance from (artificial) intelligence." It provides a comprehensive repository of the verbatim transcripts from dialogues conducted between the authors and various artificial intelligence models. These discussions pertain to the three quantum optics problems analyzed within the publication.
